Squash Social

Squash Social is now available for all Android devices with Android version 8.0 or higher, clicking the button below will redirect you to the app download.

This project came up with the main idea of being a social network where users can post, chat in private, follow other users and more.

Currently Squash Social is the main Squash application, but we are totally focused on our other products, your Squash account is unitary so you will only need to create an account on the platform and thus have access to all services.

Security and Privacy

All products distributed by Squash are governed by TOS (Terms of Service), UGC (User Generated Content) and Privacy Policy.

From the moment a user creates an account on our platform, he automatically agrees with all the rules and terms, his information is transmitted and stored encrypted and with the greatest possible security.
