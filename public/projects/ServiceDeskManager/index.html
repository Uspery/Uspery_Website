Service Desk Manager (SDM)

Idea

SDM is a project developed by Uspery on request with the aim of automating tasks performed by the Service Desk and Licensing sector.

Benefit

According to the company that ordered the SDM, it achieved an 85% increase in productivity in the sector.

- More Agility
- Better management
- Easy to generate report

Technology

The technologies used in the development of the SDM were: Windows Forms (.NET Framework 4.8) and SQL Server.

Dependencies

SDM was designed for an Own Premises Implementation having all its infrastructure within the company itself. Therefore, there is no dependency on other Applications/Servers.

Modulos Externos

SDM has the ability to receive information from ERP(s), Snow License Manager, Kaspersky and can receive and send information to Active Directory and PDQ Deploy.
